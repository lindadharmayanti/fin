Who are these courses for?

Whoever you think will benefit from them most.

Think of those people in your organisation who could help you deliver even better results if they had a solid grounding in the financial basics of business.

Some companies have sent their sales directors to us; others their lawyers, bankers or depot managers. It’s up to you – we can adapt the programme to suit all levels, roles and needs.

After all, a basic understanding of things like accounting, finance and tax is something that could benefit every decision-maker in your organisation. That way they can:

  • Make better financial decisions
  • Form stronger partnerships with your customers
  • Form stronger partnerships with your suppliers
  • Make better lending decisions
  • Have the tools to appraise a potential acquisition target
  • Understand how they can improve the profitability and cash generation of your organisation
  • Understand how management decisions impact on the results of your organisation
  • Have the confidence to ask good questions about financial information
  • Help drive the future success of the organisation

What do the courses cover?

Whatever works best for you.

Our bespoke courses – Finance Kickstart, Accounting Kickstart and Tax Kickstart – are modular, so we can cover whatever blend of subjects would best suit your needs.

We are happy to work closely with you to understand your organisational and personal needs so that we can advise you on the best range of subjects to cover.

In all cases, your learning will be supported by case studies and practical application.

Our courses seek to demystify accounting and to ensure that delegates leave with a good understanding of financial statements that they can translate to their roles.

  • Understanding a business and the importance of non-financial factors
  • Budgeting
  • Basic financial statements and the accounting process
  • Cash and the cash flow statement
  • Working capital management
  • Analysis of financial statements including ratio analysis
  • Corporation tax
  • VAT
  • Payroll taxes
  • Inheritance tax
  • Income tax
  • Funding a business
  • Commercial awareness
  • Credit skills
